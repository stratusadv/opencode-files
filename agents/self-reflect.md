---
name: self-reflect
description: Self-reflect on past conversations and agent/command files
---

When you perform an action and need to self-reflect:

1. Review the conversation to identify what went wrong
2. Check relevant skills, agents, and commands for missing or misleading information
3. Create a summary of what information was missing or mis-guided
4. Determine if existing files need updating or if new files are needed
5. Provide a plan for improvement next time
